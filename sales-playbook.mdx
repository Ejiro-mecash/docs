## Section 03 — Overview of Every meCash API

*Eight products. One integration. Infinite leverage.*

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Wallet API — Core Infrastructure" icon="wallet">
    Create, hold, and manage digital money accounts (wallets) inside any platform — like giving your software its own internal banking ledger.

    **Solves:** Without this, companies maintain error-prone Excel sheets to track which user owns what balance. The Wallet API automates the entire ledger.

    **Best For:** Fintechs · Digital Banks · Investment Apps · Crypto Exchanges · Super Apps · Gig Platforms

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Hold USD, NGN, KES, GBP simultaneously
    - Automated segregation of customer vs. corporate funds
    - Enables in-app digital balances, stored value, loyalty cashbacks
    - Generate millions of individual sub-wallets instantly

    <Tip>
      💡 **Analogy:** Like a casino chip system — the casino knows exactly how many chips are in circulation at any second.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quote API — FX & Currency" icon="calculator">
    Live guaranteed foreign exchange conversion rates that lock in the exact wholesale price for a set window of time.

    **Solves:** Currency markets fluctuate every second. Without a rate lock, merchants lose profit margins to exchange rate swings between quoting and receiving payment.

    **Best For:** Remittance · Cross-Border E-Commerce · International Payroll · Travel Platforms

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Lock in guaranteed interbank rates for up to 15 minutes
    - Zero FX slippage — meCash absorbs the volatility risk
    - Penny-perfect transparency at checkout
    - Wholesale rates — bypassing commercial bank markups

    <Tip>
      💡 **Analogy:** Like "Hold this price for 24 hours" on a flight booking — you're guaranteed the rate even if prices change.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Payout API — Disbursement" icon="arrow-right">
    An automated money-sending engine that instantly disburses individual payments to external bank accounts or mobile wallets globally.

    **Solves:** Manual payout processes require finance staff to log into bank portals, enter account numbers one-by-one, and authorize transfers individually — agonizingly slow and error-prone.

    **Best For:** Ride-Hailing · Food Delivery · Betting · Freelance Marketplaces · Insurance Claims

    **Key Benefits:**
    - 95%+ of payouts settle in under 60 seconds, 24/7/365
    - Dual-rail: bank accounts AND mobile money wallets
    - Auto-detects invalid accounts before funds are lost
    - Enables "Instant Cashout" business models

    <Tip>
      💡 **Analogy:** Like an automated ATM built inside your office — type a name, cash materializes in their account.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bulk Payout API — Mass Disbursement" icon="users">
    An industrial-strength disbursement tool — dispatches thousands of individual payments simultaneously with a single batch command.

    **Solves:** Sending 50,000 individual API calls sequentially could overload systems and take hours. Bulk Payout bundles them into one massively optimized batch.

    **Best For:** Payroll Software · Government Agencies · NGOs · Agri Co-ops · Affiliate Networks

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Process up to 100,000 transactions per run
    - Fault-tolerant: one failed payment doesn't block the rest
    - Pre-validation scrubbing before a single cent moves
    - One clean consolidated report for 10,000+ transactions

    <Tip>
      💡 **Analogy:** Like a corporate mailroom printing press — not one letter, but 100,000 simultaneously on an assembly line.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Virtual Accounts API — Collection & Reconciliation" icon="building-columns">
    Unique, programmable digital bank account numbers — real-looking accounts that route funds instantly into a master wallet with full payer identification.

    **Static vs. Dynamic:**
    - **Static VA:** Permanent account assigned forever to ONE customer (like a private P.O. Box).
    - **Dynamic VA:** Temporary, self-destructing account tied to ONE shopping cart with a timer (like a coat-check ticket).

    **Best For:** B2B Wholesale · Real Estate · Schools · Lenders · Utilities · Crypto

    **Key Benefits:**
    - 100% automated reconciliation — no manual matching ever
    - Zero chargebacks (bank transfers are irreversible)
    - Displays your company's brand name in banking apps
    - Instantly rejects or flags wrong-amount transfers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mobile Money Collection API — Mobile-First Collection" icon="mobile-screen-button">
    Directly pull or receive funds from customer mobile wallets (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Orange Money) across Africa.

    **Solves:** In sub-Saharan Africa, mobile money is the primary currency. Businesses that only accept Visa/Mastercard are actively locking out up to 85% of their potential customers.

    **Supported Networks:** MTN MoMo · M-Pesa (KE/TZ) · Vodafone Cash · AirtelTigo · Orange Money · Wave

    **Key Benefits:**
    - STK Push — secure PIN prompt directly on customer's phone
    - Works on basic $10 Nokia feature phones via USSD
    - No smartphone or internet connection required
    - Multi-country access via a single meCash contract
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transaction API — Data & Compliance" icon="receipt">
    The master financial surveillance and data retrieval engine — real-time visibility, historical tracking, and rich reporting on every financial event.

    **Solves:** When processing thousands of daily transactions, support teams, auditors, and CFOs need instant searchable access to payment history without digging through database logs.

    **Best For:** CFOs · Compliance Officers · Customer Support · Finance Controllers

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Instant ticket resolution for customer support teams
    - 7+ years of immutable, queryable transaction records
    - Filter by custom metadata, user IDs, or invoice tags
    - ERP integration-ready (SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks, Xero)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Webhooks — Real-Time Events" icon="bell">
    An automatic digital push notification that alerts your software the exact millisecond a financial event occurs — no polling required.

    **Polling vs. Webhooks:**
    - ❌ **Polling:** "Is my pizza ready? Is it ready? Is it ready?" — every 2 minutes, all day.
    - ✅ **Webhooks:** The shop texts you the exact second your driver pulls in.

    **Best For:** E-Commerce Fulfillment · Gaming & Betting · IoT & Smart Metering · Logistics · SaaS

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Cryptographically signed — completely tamper-proof
    - Auto-retry for up to 72 hours if your server goes down
    - Eliminates wasteful polling server costs
    - Trigger instant downstream actions on confirmed payment
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

    **✅ Right:** "Flutterwave handles your front-end card collection. meCash handles the back-office layer — your reconciliation, your multi-currency wallets, your bulk payouts — the infrastructure Flutterwave doesn't touch."

    Positioning meCash as a gateway replacement creates unnecessary competitive friction. We are infrastructure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mistake 4: Not quantifying the customer's current cost" icon="calculator">
    **❌ Wrong:** "This would save you a lot of time on reconciliation."

    **✅ Right:** "You said you have 4 finance staff spending 40% of their time on reconciliation. At an average salary of $1,500/month, that's $2,400/month in pure manual cost we can automate for less than $600/month in API fees."

    Always convert their pain into a number before presenting the price.
  </Accordion>
