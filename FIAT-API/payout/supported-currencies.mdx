# Supported Currencies and Payment Channels

The following table details the currencies currently supported and the corresponding payment channels available for each:

| S/N| Country | Country Code | Currency | Payment Channel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | China 🇨🇳 | CN | CNY | WeChat |
| 2 | China 🇨🇳 | CN | CNY | AliPay |
| 3 | China 🇨🇳 | CN | CNY | Bank Transfer |
| 4 | Nigeria 🇳🇬 | NG | NGN | Bank Transfer |

## Currency Details

CNY (Chinese Yuan): The official currency of the People's Republic of China. We support transactions in CNY via popular digital wallets, WeChat Pay and Alipay, as well as traditional bank transfers.
NGN (Nigerian Naira): The official currency of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Currently, bank transfers are the supported payment channel for NGN transactions.

## Payment Channel Information

- **WeChat** : A mobile payment platform widely used in China, integrated within the WeChat messaging application.
- **Alipay** : A leading third-party online payment platform in China, providing secure and convenient payment solutions.
- **Bank Transfer** : A traditional method of transferring funds directly from one bank account to another.

## Future Enhancements

We are continually expanding our supported currencies and payment channels to better serve our global user base. Future updates may include:

- Integration with additional digital wallets and payment gateways.
- Support for other major and emerging market currencies.
- Implementation of alternative payment methods.
