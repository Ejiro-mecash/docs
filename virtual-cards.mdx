## Purchasing a Virtual Card

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Select a Card">
    Navigate to the **Virtual Cards** section in your dashboard. You will see available card options, such as the **Swype Global Virtual Visa**. Click **Buy Now** on the card you wish to purchase.

    <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-2.png" alt="Select Card to Purchase" />
    <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-2.png" alt="Select Card to Purchase" />
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2. Enter Purchase Details">
    Fill in the purchase details, including:
    - **Purchase Amount**: The amount to load onto the card (in USD).
    - **Quantity**: The number of cards you wish to buy.
    - **Currency to Debit**: The wallet currency you want to pay with (e.g., NGN).

    The system will automatically calculate the exchange rate and total payable amount.

    <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-3.png" alt="Enter Purchase Details" />
    <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-3.png" alt="Enter Purchase Details" />
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3. Review Transaction">
    Review the transaction summary, which includes the purchase amount, transaction fee, card issuance fee, and the total amount to be debited from your wallet.

    <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-4.png" alt="Confirm Transaction Details" />
    <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-4.png" alt="Confirm Transaction Details" />
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4. Complete Verification">
    Enter the OTP sent to your registered email address to authorize the transaction. Once verified, your virtual card(s) will be issued instantly.

    <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-5.png" alt="Enter Verification Code" />
    <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/public/images/virtual-cards-5.png" alt="Enter Verification Code" />
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
