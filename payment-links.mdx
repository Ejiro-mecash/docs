# Payment Links

Payment Links allow you to collect one-time payments from your customers without needing a website or complex integration. You can simply create a link, share it with your customer, and get paid.

## Creating a Payment Link

To create a new payment link, follow these steps:

<details>
<summary>1. Navigate to Payment Links</summary>

On the main menu, click on **Payment Links**.
![Payment Links Menu](/images/payment-links-menu.png)
</details>

<details>
<summary>2. Initiate Creation</summary>

Click on the **+ New Payment Link** button in the top right corner.
![Create Payment Link Form](/images/create-payment-link-form.png)
</details>

<details>
<summary>3. Fill in the Details</summary>

You will be presented with a form to enter the payment details:
*   **Currency**: Select the currency for the payment (e.g., NGN).
*   **Country**: Select the country (e.g., NG).
*   **Description**: Enter a clear description for the payment (e.g., "Ejiro Fintech Course"). This helps your customers understand what they are paying for.
*   **Amount**: Enter the amount to be collected (e.g., 50,000.00).
![Filled Payment Link Form](/images/create-payment-link-filled.png)
</details>

<details>
<summary>4. Create</summary>

Once you have filled in all the fields, click the **Create Payment Link** button.
</details>

## Sharing Your Link

After successfully creating the link, you will see a success confirmation.

![Payment Link Success](/images/payment-link-success.png)

<details>
<summary>1. Copy Link</summary>

Click the **Copy Link** button to copy the unique payment URL to your clipboard.
</details>

<details>
<summary>2. Share</summary>

Paste this link in an email, WhatsApp message, or social media post to send it to your customer.
</details>

## Managing Payment Links

You can view and manage all your created payment links from the **Payment Links** dashboard.

![Payment Links List](/images/payment-links-list.png)

*   **View List**: The dashboard shows a list of all your payment links, including the Description, Payment Link URL, Date Created, Amount, and Status.
*   **Status**: Check if a link is **Active** or inactive.
*   **Actions**: Use the **Manage** button to view more details or perform actions on a specific link.
