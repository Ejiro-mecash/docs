USD Virtual Accounts allow you to receive US Dollar payments directly into your meCash wallet. These accounts are ideal for businesses and individuals who need to collect payments in USD.

### **Supported Corridors**

- **United States (USD)**

## Request Example

To create a USD virtual account, you need to provide the customer's details along with the company information.

<CodeGroup>

{
  "currency": "USD",
  "country": "US",
  "firstName": "John",
  "lastName": "Mary",

  "reference": "REF_8899562444KLLTTPL",
  "accountName": "Test Ng Ltd",
  "gender": "M",
  "dateOfBirth": "1980-10-10",
  "nationality": "NG",
