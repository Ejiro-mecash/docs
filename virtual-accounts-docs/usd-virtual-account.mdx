USD Virtual Accounts allow you to receive US Dollar payments directly into your meCash wallet. These accounts are ideal for businesses and individuals who need to collect payments in USD.

### **Supported Corridors**

- **United States (USD)**

## Request Example

To create a USD virtual account, you need to provide the customer's details along with the company registration number.
